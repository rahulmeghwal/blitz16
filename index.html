    BLITZSCHLAG '16

    The annual Tech-cult fest of MNIT Jaipur is fast approaching. Since its inception, Blitzschlag has epitomized the ultimate array of technical and cultural activities on an Institute platform. Youth from the entire nation is held incaptivating by the colourful milieu of technical and cultural events that team Blitzschlag conscientiously puts together for them to have the time of their lives.

    Blitzschlag has been an utopia for the young generation for them to nurture and showcase their cultural and managerial skills. In the recent past, Blitzschlag has witnessed the riproaring performance of Parikrama, the thrilling MTV Stunt Mania, campus attack by UTV Bindass and mega nights including the electrifying Ramba Samba, the exciting Panache, the hard-core Ragna Rock and the traditional Classical Night. We have a wide assortment of competitions that’ll cater to everyone’s expectations ranging from robolympics and junkyard wars to face painting and junky costumes, from debates to Dalal Street, quizzes to war of the DJs. Blitzschlag has always persevered to make each of its event a delectable delight. You are all invited to come and enjoy this 3 day extravaganza and win exciting prizes. So mates, gear yourself because its now time for Thunder and Lightning!
